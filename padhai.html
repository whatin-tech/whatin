About WHATIN

Hello! My name is Dushyant Saini, and I am the founder of WHATIN. Our vision is simple yet powerful: to provide every student with easy access to academic resources, regardless of their year or course. At WHATIN, we are dedicated to offering AKTU University previous year papers (PYPs) and other study materials that can help students excel in their academic journey. Our mission is to simplify learning by ensuring that students have all the resources they need in one place, making preparation for exams more effective, structured, and stress-free.

We understand that navigating through university exams can be challenging. Students often face difficulties in finding authentic, relevant, and organized content that aligns with their syllabus. WHATIN addresses this problem by providing carefully curated previous year papers from AKTU University, covering all courses and all years. These papers are designed to help students practice thoroughly, understand exam patterns, and gain confidence before attempting their own exams. By giving access to past questions, we allow learners to identify important topics, revise efficiently, and enhance their problem-solving skills.

Our platform is not just about papers; it is about empowering students. We aim to provide clarity, direction, and motivation in their academic journey. By offering free access to PYPs and study content, WHATIN ensures that every student, whether in the first year or the final year, can achieve their full potential without facing any financial or accessibility barriers. We believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone, and our platform reflects this commitment.

In addition to academic preparation, WHATIN encourages students to develop a deeper understanding of their subjects, explore new ideas, and build a strong foundation for their careers. Each paper and study material we provide is carefully organized, original, and designed to guide students in achieving academic excellence. Our goal is to give learners not just resources, but also a sense of hope, direction, and confidence for their future.

In essence, WHATIN is more than a study platform; it is a trusted companion for every AKTU student, providing structured resources, past exam papers, and guidance to help them succeed. We strive to create an ecosystem where students can access everything they need for their academic journey in one place, making learning simpler, more organized, and highly effective.
