Pro Tips for Success

Aadhaar Seeding is Crucial

Ensure your Bank Account is seeded with Aadhaar and NCPB mapping is active. Most rejections happen due to "Aadhaar Not Seeded" status.

Income Certificate Validity

Always check the issuance date. UP Govt requires the certificate to be within the last 3 years at the time of final submission.

Final Print Submission

After locking the application, wait for 3 days to get the final print. Submit this print with all attachments to your college office promptly.
