Frequently Asked Questions

Which certificate should I choose?
Choose Domicile to confirm state residence (essential for UP Scholarship), Caste for reservation benefits, or Income to prove eligibility for financial allowances and fee waivers.
How is the application handled?
Our student-centric coordination team reviews your uploaded documents for standard compliance, formats them to government criteria, files the application, and retrieves the verified public PDF resource for you.
What if my application gets rejected?
If the state department raises an objection (e.g. blur scans), our Fast Track program features an immediate objection correction. Standard applications are notified for re-uploading documents.
Are my documents secure?
Yes, WHATIN adheres to strict confidentiality rules. Once the credential department releases your certificate, local instances of uploaded private applicant files are removed from our storage systems.
