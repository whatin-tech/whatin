Are these notes sufficient for AKTU exams?

Yes, our Topper Notes are specifically designed to cover 100% of the AKTU syllabus with a focus on high-probability questions. However, we recommend practicing with PYPs for best results.

How often is the archive updated?

We update our archive every semester, usually within 15 days of the latest university examinations. Always check the "Last Updated" badge on each page.

Is WHATIN really free to use?

Absolutely! Our mission is to democratize education. All PDF resources, guidance articles, and notes are 100% free for all students. We are supported by community contributions and minimal ads.

What is the "Dev Zone" for?

The Dev Zone is an interactive playground where students can experiment with coding logic, play educational games, and explore the technical side of the WHATIN ecosystem.

What makes WHATIN different?

Unlike other scattered repositories, we provide a unified "Student Help Ecosystem." We combine verified notes, strategic roadmaps, and career guidance in one premium, ad-light interface.

Can I contribute my own notes?

Absolutely! We love student contributions. Reach out via the contact form with your subject name and a link to your verified study material to help the community grow.
