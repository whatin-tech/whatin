Student Affidavit Portal

College document verifications made easy. Apply for Anti-Ragging, Gap Certificates, and other official affidavits smoothly.

Available Affidavits

Anti Ragging

Mandatory affidavit for all students declaring strict adherence to anti-ragging policies of the institution and UGC.

Gap Affidavit

Required if you have a study gap between your previous qualification and current admission. Validates your activities.

Scholarship Affidavit

Income declaration or no-objection affidavit required for applying to various state and central government scholarship schemes.

Name Correction

Father/Mother name problem affidavit to legally declare the correct names in case of discrepancies in official documents.

Mandatory Document

Aadhar Cards Strictly Required

For complete legal identity verification on the stamp paper, clear photocopies (both sides) of Aadhar Cards are absolutely mandatory for the following individuals:

Yourself
Your Father / Mother
Your Grandfather

Frequently Asked Questions

How exactly does this process work?

First, select the type of affidavit you need. Then, fill out the form with your details, submit your documents, and complete the payment. After that, choose your preferred delivery mode (Fast or Normal). Finally, your legally notarized affidavit will be delivered directly to you inside the college campus.

Is this service reliable? Can I trust it?

Yes, it is 100% reliable and verified. There is absolutely a 0% chance of any scam. Your documents are handled professionally, and the final affidavit is legally valid and stamped by an authorized Notary.

What if I provide the wrong details in the form?

You are strictly responsible for the details you submit. However, if you provide wrong information and mistakenly complete the payment, contact the host immediately. You can find the host's direct number in our official WhatsApp group. Get your details corrected on time before printing. If printed with errors, the liability falls entirely on you.

What is the difference between Fast and Normal mode?

In Fast Delivery mode, you pay an extra ₹30 and your affidavit will be ready within 1 to 2 days. In Normal mode, the standard processing time is about 1 week. This is the primary difference between the two modes.

What if my father is absent or deceased? Whose Aadhar should I provide?

By default, your Father's Aadhar card is strictly required. However, in the unfortunate event that your father is deceased or absent, you can absolutely provide the Aadhar card of your Mother or legal Guardian instead.
