Bridging the Employment Gap

Finding the right internship or job is the logical next step after mastering the academic curriculum. The WHATIN Careers Sandbox uses advanced API integrations to pull real-time job data, ensuring you have access to live opportunities right within your study environment.

Secure API Integration

Your security is our priority. We route all job fetch requests through a Serverless Vercel Backend Proxy. This architectural decision ensures our API keys remain completely hidden from the client-side network payload, preventing malicious actors from hijacking our quotas while giving you a seamless, secure browsing experience.
